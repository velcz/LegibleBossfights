[h3]Lightweight mod that attempts to make it easier to see whats going on during bossfights.[/h3]

[list]
[*]Adds a line that connects the player to the cursor to help with aiming.
[*]Highlights enemy projectiles to make them easier to see.
[*]Optionally makes friendly projectiles transparent to reduce visual clutter.
[/list]

All features can be toggled on/off and adjusted individually.
